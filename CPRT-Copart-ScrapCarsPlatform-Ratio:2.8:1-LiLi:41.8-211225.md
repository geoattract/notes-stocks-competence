## SECTION 1: BUSINESS MOAT & COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

### Core Business (One Sentence)

* Copart provides online auction and vehicle remarketing services for salvage and used vehicles, primarily for insurance companies.

### Durable Competitive Advantage - STRICT TEST

* **PASS:** Copart possesses a massive two-sided network effect (global buyer base + insurance supply) and a physical "moat" through its extensive ownership of licensed salvage land, which faces extreme zoning hurdles for new entrants.
* Does the company have pricing power? **YES.** It has consistently raised service fees and maintained margins despite competitive pressures.
* Is this moat widening, stable, or narrowing? **WIDENING.** Copart has steadily gained market share from its primary competitor, IAA (now RB Global), due to its superior technology and logistics network.
